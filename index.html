Historic Inspiration

Nestled since 1254 at the foot of Box Hill, our Grade II–listed coaching inn blends centuries of literary and aristocratic heritage with modern luxury. Once a retreat for Lord Nelson and an inspiration for poets like John Keats, the Sunday Mercure Box Hill Burford Bridge Hotel offers a serene escape in the Surrey Hills. Enjoy elegant rooms, fine dining at Emlyn Restaurant, and tranquil riverside gardens, perfect for leisure, weddings, or business.

Rooms & Suites

Classic, Superior & Privilege

Discover 57 beautifully appointed bedrooms, each blending historic charm with modern comforts. Classic rooms offer cozy elegance, Superior rooms provide extra space with scenic views of the Surrey countryside, and Privilege rooms deliver unmatched sophistication with historic touches. All rooms feature luxurious bedding, high-speed Wi-Fi, and serene views, ensuring a restful stay.

Lido

Seasonal Outdoor Pool

From July to September, our seasonal outdoor pool offers a refreshing escape under the Surrey sky. Nestled by the River Mole, the Lido is surrounded by lush gardens, perfect for lounging or enjoying a cocktail from our poolside service. Towels and loungers are provided for your comfort, making it an ideal spot for relaxation.

Meetings & Events

Business with Distinction

Host unforgettable events in our historic venues. The medieval Tithe Barn accommodates up to 180 guests with its original beams and charm. The bright Garden Room, with a private patio and croquet lawn, is ideal for up to 70 guests. The riverside Burford Room offers serene views for smaller gatherings. All spaces include air conditioning, integrated screens, sound systems, and bespoke catering.

Getting Here

Easily accessible from London and beyond, our hotel is located at the foot of Box Hill in Surrey.

By Train: Nearest stations are Box Hill & Westhumble (1 mile) and Dorking (2 miles), with regular services from London Waterloo and Victoria (approximately 50 minutes).

By Bus: Bus 465 connects Dorking and Kingston, with a stop near the hotel for convenient access.

By Car: Complimentary on-site parking is available. Find us off the A24 at Old London Road, Mickleham, Dorking, RH5 6BX.
